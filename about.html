ABOUT CORONET OUR MANAGEMENT AWARDS & ACHIEVEMENTS

In the year of 2000, Coronet Engineers Pvt. Ltd. was originally established by the incomparable experienced group of engineers and an organization evolved by its heritage in FACTORY AUTOMATION SYSTEM which strengthen the organization to offer a wide variety of automation products and engineering services to its customers, is a leading, trusted & most admired automation company in the field of process and power automation. Synergies of CORONET with experienced and highly skilled staff and partners now increased its business portfolio by diversifying in to the new dimensions becoming a perfect partner for ELECTRICAL, INSTRUMENTATION & AUTOMATION need of any Industrial & Infrastructural segment. We offer integrated total turnkey as well as product solutions that lead to better business processes and more efficiency for customers. Our focus is on operational excellence through the design and delivery of innovative, sustainable and resilient solutions for our clients. As a broad-based technical services provider we meet all our clients' needs, from consultancy and design of electrical systems to through to the installation and whole-life maintenance and management of these assets CORONET, being the most suitable partner in technical services, which excels in the realisation of integrated, sustainable and innovative solutions in the area of technology and maintenance with a demonstrable value for its customers Our vision and efforts are focused on the true need of customer which derive a high quality solutions with aim to provide total solution from a single point of contact.

Backed by Technocrats & professional management team that brings to the table years of technical and business expertise, CORONET has served one satisfied client after the other. Veterans in the field of process control, plant & energy management and automation, our team has enabled the company to gain a strong foothold in the industry.
